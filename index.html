Our flavors

We offer 3 ways of implementing our client SDK:

Complete payment flow tool: by adding just a piece of code and a few configurations, get a full payment flow running in your app.

Built-in UI: customize your payment flow using SDK's provided UI to simplify data collection.

Customizable UI: complete customizable payment flow using MercadoPago SDK's methods to retrieve data.
